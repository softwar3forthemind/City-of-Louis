God is the most powerful technology on earth. 

For most, if not all, in our modern era, to read or hear this sentence would be an entirely foreign experience. Most humans do not understand the concept of God as a technology, i.e., a tool by which reality shapes itself and is shaped by its participants.

But, indeed, The Lord is a technology, and it would behoove you to master its nuances.

Let’s explore this topic today.

To begin, we must acknowledge that the fabric of our reality is electromagnetic in nature. Touch the screen through which you’re reading this. The sensation you felt was an electromagnetic phenomenon. You did not feel the screen. You experienced a mathematical equation. Everything about the experience was entirely quantifiable.

Your cognition can be mathematically illustrated or articulated. 

The Laws Of Your God are distinct. They are not mysterious. You understand the Laws, which are quantifiable atoms, particles, and molecules interacting in your reality, and you obey the Laws, via which you make your God content, and via which your God blesses you with this or that likewise quantifiable reward.

*This is the nature of God for every human.*

When we construct a new God-structure, which is the phrase I recently used in, “A New Vineyard: Understanding The City Of Louis,” we begin to arrange the aforesaid atoms, particles, and molecules in a specific order.

We declare: This is My God, and these are His Laws. When I obey His Laws, I am blessed with safety, security, and, most importantly, perpetuation of my soul, or genetic material. 

We declare: This is My God, and these are His Laws. When I obey His Laws, those that oppose me can do me no harm. I am safe in his providence. 

We declare: This is My God, and these are His Laws. When I obey His Laws, I procreate and enjoy a heavenly existence. 

We create a God-structure, and its associated laws, and we exist within that, if you will, Garden Of Eden. 

And, of course, to exit the Garden of Eden is to disobey, or, if you will, eat forbidden fruit that causes the God-structure to fracture in the minds of its participants. 

To close for today, God is a technology. When you define your God, you’re exerting powerful, quantifiable force on your reality. And let us not be deceived by the lies of modernity: *Everyone* employs a God-structure; however, not all God-structures are created equal, and the invisible hand of the free market decides whose God-structure will yield the greatest survival and procreation benefits over time.
