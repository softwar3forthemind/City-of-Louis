THE CYCLE OF EDEN
Illustrating the Life, Death & Resurrection of God-Structures
EVIL INFILTRATES
via new or opposing thought — corrupting God's Law & the covenant
RENEWAL
Christ consciousness is renewed
RE-ENTRY

EDEN
The enduring covenant with God and His law.

The Devil, or Lucifer, the Light-Bringer, infiltrates Eden and convinces its leading woman to imbibe his new ideas, creating psychological discord in Eden. We might imagine this as the Protestant Reformation — creating further schisms, destroying the God-structure of Catholic Europe, which today lies in complete tatters.
