The Author

About

Louis Stevens

City of Louis takes its name from 2 Samuel 5:7 — "Nevertheless, David captured the fortress of Zion — which is the City of David." It is the first moment in scripture in which a place is named after its author.

As David conquered and consecrated Zion, making it the seat of his kingdom and the dwelling place of God's presence, City of Louis is a conquered and consecrated space of thought — a promised land entered by those who apply the philosophies discussed herein.

City of Louis is devoted to authoring a better society, as we enter the age of Artificial Intelligence, Space Exploration, and Virtual Reality.

City of Louis is not affiliated with any government, institution, organization, or entity of any kind. It is the independent work of one individual.
