The Author

      
About


      
Louis Stevens


      

        
City of Louis is an independent digital publication operated by Louis Stevens, a sole proprietor based in Florida. It publishes original written commentary across four editorial pillars: Law, Capital, Technology, and New Adam. New works are produced and published regularly, delivered exclusively through this platform. Reader support is accepted through voluntary patronage contributions — a content subscription service. No physical goods are sold. All transactions are processed securely through Stripe.

        
City of Louis is a path toward a new version of society — one authored deliberately, through the rigorous application of philosophy to law, capital, and technology. Named after 2 Samuel 5:7, in which David names Zion after himself and makes it the seat of his kingdom, City of Louis is a space in which a new civilization of mind is built, entry by entry, one reader at a time.
